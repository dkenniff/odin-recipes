Monkey Beer

Description

Monkey beer is a delicious mysterious drink made by the monkey Frank hired to bartend at Paddy's Pub.

Indredients

  • Beer
  • Whiskey
  • Monkey

Steps

  1. Hire a monkey as a bartender
  2. Pour a glass of beer
  3. Pour half a bottle of whiskey into the glass a beer
  4. Serve and enjoy!
